Drum-set Shopping List

In the list below I will be detailing the items needed when buying an intermediate or pro drum kit. As you move up in quality on these sets, you'll notice they start to be referenced more as shells, because, the shells are sold independently of the rest of the set.

  • Drum shells
  • Drum heads
  • Drum hardware
  • Bass drum pedals
  • Cymbals
  • Drum sticks

In this second unordered list I will elaberate and list the hardware mentioned in the above list.

  • Hi-hat stand
  • Cymbal stand
  • Tom mount stands
  • Ride cymbal stand
